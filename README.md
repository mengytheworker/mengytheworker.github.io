# Wecome to Meng Yu's homepage

This is my, Meng Yu's, homepage. Welcome! I have been trying to bring radio astronomy to the public. This page is where I present my work.

There are now two topics: radio pulsars and sky brightness temperatures. To help you have a first overall impression, I show two maps on the Pulsar page and the 408MHz page; the maps show all-sky's 3,319 known radio pulsars or the sky's brightness temperatures respectively. What is more exciting is I've also brought you real-observations' simulations. The App page has the links to the iOS apps. With the apps, you can do the observations on your phone!

In my spare time, I also created a little game. You find it on the Fun page. It won't let you down!

On the Tip page, I share my coding experiences with you. Wish you enjoying coding too!

My acknowledgement is under the Thanks page.
